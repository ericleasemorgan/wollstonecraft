it is the nature of man, either in a savage state or living in society, to protect his property; and it is wise in a government to encourage this spirit. for the example now displayed by france is a notable proof of the inexpediency of standing armies, so long as the people have an interest in supporting the political system under which they live. the national assembly, aware of this, invited the militia and the municipalities, to endeavour to quell the disorders which did violence to persons and property;
