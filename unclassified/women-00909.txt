as a sex, women are more chaste than men, and as modesty is the effect of chastity, they may deserve to have this virtue ascribed to them in rather an appropriated sense; yet, i must be allowed to add an hesitating if:  for i doubt whether chastity will produce modesty, though it may propriety of conduct, when it is merely a respect for the opinion of the world, and when coquetry and the love-lorn tales of novelists employ the thoughts. nay, from experience, and reason, i should be led to expect to meet with more modesty amongst men than women, simply because men exercise their understandings more than women.
