but what have women to do in society? i may be asked, but to loiter with easy grace; surely you would not condemn them all to suckle fools and chronicle small beer!a no. women might certainly study the art of healing, and be physicians as well as nurses. and midwifery, decency seems to allot to them, though i am afraid the word midwife, in our dictionaries, will soon give place to accoucheur,b and one proof of the former delicacy of the sex be effaced from the language.
