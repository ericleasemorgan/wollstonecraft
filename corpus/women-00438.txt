it is not impious thus to scan the attributes of the almighty: in fact, who can avoid it that exercises his faculties? for to love god as the fountain of wisdom, goodness, and power, appears to be the only worship useful to a being who wishes to acquire either virtue or knowledge. a blind unsettled affection may, like human passions, occupy the mind and warm the heart, whilst, to do justice, love mercy, and walk humbly with our god, is forgotten. i shall pursue this subject still further, when i consider religion in a light opposite to that recommended by dr gregory, who treats it as a matter of sentiment or taste.a/
