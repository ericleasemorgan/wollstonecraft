in nurseries, and boarding-schools, i fear, girls are first spoiled; particularly in the latter. a number of girls sleep in the same room, and wash together. and, though i should be sorry to contaminate an innocent creature's mind by instilling false delicacy, or those indecent prudish notions, which early cautions respecting the other sex naturally engender, i should be very anxious to prevent their acquiring nasty, or immodest habits; and as many girls have learned very nasty tricks, from ignorant servants, the / mixing them thus indiscriminately together, is very improper.
