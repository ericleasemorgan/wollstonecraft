liberty, in this simple, unsophisticated sense, i acknowledge, is a fair idea that has never yet received a form in the various governments that have been established on our beauteous globe; the demon of property has ever been at hand to encroach on the sacred rights of men, and to fence round with awful pomp laws that war with justice. but that it results from the eternal foundation of right  from immutable truth  who will presume to deny, that pretends to rationality  if reason has led them to build their morality and religion on / an everlasting foundation  the attributes of god?
